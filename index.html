So how do I use this, anyway?

Pencil tool: Click on a cell to fill it in, right-click to delete (shift-click works too). You can also drag across the grid to quickly fill in or delete cells.

Paint bucket tool: Click on a cell to fill in all like cells with the current color. Right- or shift-click to clear all like cells.

Ctrl-Z (or Command-Z) will undo the last action.
